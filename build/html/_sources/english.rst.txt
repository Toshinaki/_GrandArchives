
英语单词
============

单词结构
--------------

前缀 (prefix) + 词根 (root) + 后缀 (suffix)

前缀 ⭐
    表达情感 (emotion) 或方向 (orientation)
词根
    本源本意 (substance)
后缀
    词性 (function)

前缀
~~~~~~~~~

↑
    suf-
        sufficient

    sup-
        superior

    sur-

    sus-
        sustain

    up-
        uphold

        upsurge

        uproar

    over-
        overlap

↓
    sub-
        substance

    suc-

    hyp-
        hyp'nosis

        hy'pocrisy (critic)

    dis-

    cat-
        catalogue

        catastrophe

←
    post-
        postpone

        post-graduate

    re-

    with-

→
    pre-
        prepaid

        preterm

        predetermine

↓ 恶意的
    mal-
        malice

        malicious

词根
~~~~~~~~~~~~

元音可以随意变换

star- (astr, aster, astro)
    'astronaut

    as'tronomy

    disaster

    catastrophe

sen- (senior)
    senility

    seniority

    seneschal

词性
~~~~~~~~~~

-ive 抽象adj.
    preservative

-ity 抽象n.
    personality

    productivity

-ate 厚道之人
    intimate

    classmate

-or / -er 主动的人

-ee 被动的人
    employee

    refugee

-ist 专家
    physicist

    specialist

-al 专业人士
    professional

-ian 在某方面执着并有兴趣的人
    politician

-ard 贱人

-itis 炎症
    appendicitis

-ia 消极的 病
    euthanasia

    hypochondria

    phobia
